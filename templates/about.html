Meet Our Team

Redina Kolaneci

Founder & Managing Director

Redina is our fundraising virtuoso who makes Christian charities thrive and their dreams come alive! Armed with a heart that beats for Kingdom causes and a brain that creates robust strategies, she is the cheerleader of Christian fundraisers everywhere.

Think of her as the guardian angel of Christian fundraising, spreading joy and inspiring positive action wherever she goes. With a twinkle in her eyes and a knack for helping you to see your ministry through the supporter's eyes, she will encourage you to think of fundraising like a fantastic, faith-filled journey rather than a wearisome task.

Sergio Mascheroni

Creative Services Director

Sergio is our creative wizard and the mastermind behind our vibrant fundraising and marketing campaigns! With a heart full of ideas and a mind that dances to the rhythm of creativity, he sprinkles joy and brilliance on whatever he does.

For Sergio, turning the ordinary into extraordinary is not just a job: it's all about making your charity's story unforgettable and your brand sparkle!

Sally Gray

Fundraising Reports Services

Sally is our fundraising data expert! With a superpower for transforming spreadsheets into success stories, she can unravel mysteries, and map out new steps to engage supporter's hearts and unlock their generosity towards your mission.

Daniela Toso

Multimedia Production & Editing

Daniela is an outstanding creative professional with over three decades of experience in design and video making. Renowned for her exceptional creative skills, she effortlessly blends visuals with the perfect music and rhythm, creating captivating and memorable videos and adverts.

David Potter

Graphic Design

David is an accomplished graphic designer, who has created many eye-catching pieces for Christian charities and organisations dedicated to doing good. A proud alumnus of the Kent Institute of Art and Design, where he honed his skills in Illustration, Dave's work exhibits to the power of visual storytelling in fostering connections and inspiring action.
